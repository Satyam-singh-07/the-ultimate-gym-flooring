STRETCH CEILING

3-5mm waterproof

Acoustic membrane, seamless finish, reduces echo – perfect for studios.

Our Stretch Ceilings are engineered from high-grade PVC polymers that offer exceptional acoustic damping, essential for high-intensity gym environments and yoga studios alike. The membrane is fire-rated (Class A) and completely moisture-resistant, preventing any mold growth. Its seamless application provides a futuristic, clean look that hides all wiring and ductwork while remaining easily accessible for maintenance.

RUNNING TRACK

8mm energy return

EPDM granules, high resilience, athletic grip for sprints & sleds.

Designed for explosive performance, our professional-grade running tracks utilize a dual-layer vulcanized system. The top layer provides maximum traction and spike resistance, while the honeycomb base layer acts as an energy-return cushion, protecting athletes' joints during sprints and plyo drills. UV-stable and weather-resistant, it's suitable for both indoor sprint corridors and outdoor functional zones.

⚡ 3 layers ★★★★☆

SPORTS GRASS

40mm pile infill option

Turf for functional fitness, indoor/outdoor, realistic feel & drainage.

This isn't just landscaping turf; it's high-density sports flooring. With a 40mm pile height and a reinforced backing, our sports grass is built to withstand heavy sled pushes and constant lateral movement without fraying. The polyethylene fibers are soft to the touch to prevent turf burns but tough enough for industrial use. Features an advanced drainage system for outdoor installation.

⚡ uv stable ★★★★★

6mm / 8mm stain resistant

Our signature White Rubber Roll is the pinnacle of gym aesthetics. Made from 100% virgin rubber, it provides a non-porous surface that is completely stain-resistant and easy to sanitize. Extremely durable and non-marking even under heavy power racks.
